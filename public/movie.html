The GOAT moives according to Cameron!

My top 3 GOATs!

The Nightmare Before Christmas

Being quite the lover of Halloween, and a not so great lover of Christmas. This movie is a good time as the citizens of Halloween town attempt to spookify Christmas. Plus is a great musical as well

Labyrinth

Staring one of my favorite musicians, David Bowie, this surreal trip into the land of the goblin king is a wild ride. It uses old school puppetry to bring a lot of the supporting cast alive.

The Super Mario Bros Movie (2023)

Nintendo's first dip of their toes into making a full scale movie. It was truly a master piece in animation and I would say one of the leading films that have broken the video game movie curse.
